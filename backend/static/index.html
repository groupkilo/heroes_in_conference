A Computer Lab Group Project 2019




Our Project

Even the best conferences and conventions experience a slump where engagement declines. We're prototyping a mobile AR experience to keep conference attendees interested - using gamification and social techniques to bring attendees together, predict and counter the slump, and deliver key information about their event.

Get Our App

Our app is available for download as a .apk file. We've populated it with information about the 2019 group project show, so download it and try it out! We'll be up in the Intel lab with a collection of AR puzzles you can have a crack at.
